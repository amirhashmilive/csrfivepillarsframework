The spectrum along which institutional claims move from pure proclamation (claims with no evidence) to verified proof (claims fully substantiated by independently verifiable evidence). Most institutions operate at position three. The Skeptical Observer Standard, position five, is the bar the Five Pillars Framework exists to meet.
