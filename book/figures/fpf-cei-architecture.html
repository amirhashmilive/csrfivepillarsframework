CEI — A SINGLE DIAGNOSTIC SCORE MEASURING INSTITUTIONAL EFFECTIVENESS
The CEI combines weighted delivery performance (WDC), stakeholder alignment (SAI), and a legitimacy multiplier (L) into a single diagnostic score. Institutions with low legitimacy cannot achieve high CEI regardless of delivery performance — the Legitimacy Factor enforces the principle that legitimacy is foundational, not additive.
