The Five Pillars Framework
The A-E-I-O-M Architecture for Institutional Legitimacy
A
Accountability
Answerable for outcomes, not activities
E
Execution
Architecture converting intent to reality
I
Initiative
Participatory origin, not imposed intent
O
Outcome
Demonstrable, attributable change
M

The Trust Layer — Verification Infrastructure
Each pillar presupposes the previous. The architecture is complete at five.
